# Citrus Image Generation Prompts

These prompts were used as project-local guidance for the generated visual direction. Current app assets live in `src/assets/backgrounds/` as PNG fallbacks and can later be replaced by compressed `.webp` or `.avif` files with the same visual intent.

## Hero / Horizon

Warm atmospheric sunrise horizon scene, distant mountain ridge and city lights far below, one bright polar-star light point above the horizon, sense of movement and civic impact without politics. Cinematic photorealistic AI image, premium mobile app visual, realistic landscape, vertical mobile composition, wide negative space in upper third for UI text, horizon in lower third. Warm cream, gold, pale blue dawn light, subtle mist. No logos, no text, no watermark, no black/darkmode look, no simple circle gradients.

## Voices / Direction

Realistic atmospheric scene of many subtle light trails across a warm urban landscape converging toward one direction, symbolizing voices becoming movement. Cinematic photorealistic premium app background, foreground road or plaza lines leading into distance, early morning cream-gray sky, gold and pale violet light accents. No readable text, no logos, no political signage, no dark gaming look, no simple circles or blob gradients.

## City / Public Movement

Premium realistic city horizon and public square at sunrise, glass architecture, distant skyline, warm light, sense of public movement and progress without politics. Vertical mobile composition, foreground plaza or steps and skyline, strong depth. Warm cream-gray sunrise, subtle gold, blue and rose lens light, high-end app aesthetic. No logos, no text, no watermark.

## Internal / Community

Realistic modern campus courtyard and warm interior-community feeling, people only as distant silhouettes, glass and stone architecture, subtle light beams suggesting collaboration. Vertical mobile composition, foreground walkway and seating, depth toward modern campus building, morning light, warm cream and pale blue. No readable text, no logos, no identifiable faces, no political imagery.

## Impact / Profile

Warm high-altitude horizon with one luminous star point and soft atmospheric depth, subtle lines of light suggesting influence and reach. Premium mobile app hero card background, bright off-white and warm gray base, gold and rose accents, not darkmode. No text, no logos, no people close-up.
